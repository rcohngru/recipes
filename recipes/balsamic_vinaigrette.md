# Balsamic Vinaigrette
[Dressings](../lists/dressings_marinades_sauces_salsas.md)

### Ingredients
- 1/4C Olive oil
- 1/4C High quality balsamic vinegar
- 1T Dijon mustard
- 1T Maple syrup or honey

### Directions
1. Combine all ingredients in a jar to taste and shake vigorously.

### Notes
- Honestly, I never follow the actual amounts and just do what smells/tastes good.
